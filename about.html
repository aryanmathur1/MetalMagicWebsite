Who We Are

A student-built team with serious drive.

Metal Magic is a dedicated FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) team from Ashburn, Virginia, founded in early 2023. Our rookie season was CENTERSTAGE (2023-2024), and our team is made up of passionate 9th to 11th grade students from Ashburn and nearby communities. This is our third year, and we are excited for the upcoming season! As we continue our journey in robotics, we look forward to learning, strengthening our skills, and facing new FTC challenges as a united team.
