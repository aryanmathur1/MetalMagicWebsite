01

2025 Academic Year Camp

Four-week camp where students formed mock robotics teams and learned about FIRST, hardware, software, and outreach with Metal Magic members as mentors.

02

2025-26 Decode Kickoff Event

In partnership with Curie Learning, we conducted a kickoff event for the 2025-26 FTC Decode Season with activities and a game reveal livestream.

03

Robotics Summer Camp 2025

Our first in-person summer camp with Curie Learning. Students built and programmed their own FTC robot in a week, then showcased it to an audience.

04

Code Crush Hackathon

Our first annual 24-hour coding competition. Students built a game or project around a theme, with bootcamps in Python, Java, and AI.

Visit Code Crush Hackathon
05

ACL Maker Showcase

At Academies of Loudoun's Maker Showcase, we shared FIRST and competitive robotics with educators, parents, and students through robot demo runs.

06

Online Robotics Bootcamp

With Curie Learning, we covered FTC basics, hardware, 3D modeling, Python, programming, marketing, logo design, and a live robot demo.

07

Library Events

Open events with stations for 3D printing, Java programming, robot driving, AI experiments, and hands-on hardware challenges.
